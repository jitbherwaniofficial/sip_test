    Idli : A South Indian Classic Breakfast

    Idli is a popular South Indian delicacy that is served as a staple breakfast item in South Indian households. It is loved for its light, fluffy texture & subtle delicious taste. Traditionally it is made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal and served with sambar and coconut chutney. This dish is cherished not only for its simplicity and ease of digestion but also for being a healthy, gluten-free option rich in protein and carbohydrates.

    We maintain the authenticity of this traditional cuisine by preparing them using a traditional recipe that’s been perfected over time. Enjoy our fluffy idlis with delicious sambhar and chutneys!!

    Meduvada : A Crispy South Indian Snack

    Meduvada is a traditional South Indian doughnut-shaped snack. It is also known as Urad Dal Vada. The crispy exterior and soft, fluffy interior is for what meduvada cherished. It is made from a batter of urad dal (black gram), which is soaked, ground, and seasoned with spices like cumin, black pepper, and curry leaves. This batter is then carved into doughnut shape and then deep-fried to get a golden and crispy exterior. Meduvada is often enjoyed as a breakfast item or snack and typically served with coconut chutney and sambar.

    We use the traditional techniques and recipe to serve you the authentic crispy Meduvadas. Order and enjoy our crunchy Meduvadas with delicious coconut chutney!!
